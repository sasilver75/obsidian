A military mission focused on physically destroying enemy air-defense systems, rather than only suppressing or disrupting them.

Targets include:
- Surface to air missile launchers
- Radar systems
- Fire-control systems
- Air-defense command posts
- Communication nodes
- Anti-aircraft artillery
- Support vehicles or reload vehicles

c.f. [[Suppression of Enemy Air Defenses|SEAD]], which simply temporarily reduces/disrupts enemy air-defense.
