Nadir point directly below a [[Satellite]], on Earth.

Taskable satellites can point [[Nadir|Off-Nadir]], though this involves increased distortion, tilt. The "[[Look Angle]]" is the degrees by which you're looking off-Nadir.

"[[Slew]]" refers to the process of rotating the satellite (changing its [[Attitude]]) to point at a new target; the slew rate limits how fast you can re-task. An [[Attitude Determination and Control System]] determines how quickly you can [[Task|Retask]] a satellite.
