*"Capella builds the worlds most advanced [[Synthetic Aperture Radar|SAR]] satellites to unlock global insight, accelerating decisions that protect people, assets, and the planet.*

Compare with other commercial imagery providers:
- [[Maxar]] (largely optical VHR)
- [[Planet Labs|Planet]] (largely optical with great coverage)
- [[Satellogic]] (affordable hyperspectral)
- [[ICEYE]] (World's largest commercial SAR fleet, all X-Band)

Founded in 2016 in San Francisco, their specialization is a commercial [[X-Band]] (finest, smallest wavelength, highest detail) [[Synthetic Aperture Radar|SAR]] [[Constellation]].
- Gives highest resolution at manageable antenna size, excellent for urban, infrastructure, vehicles. Small antennas result in smaller/cheaper satellites.
- Less penetration, can't see through canopy of soil like [[L-Band]] (e.g. [[PALSAR]]), less useful for forest biomass, soil moisture.

Available Constellations (named after mountains/peaks)

2. Acadia (2023): ~7 satellites, 0.25m resolution
