In the US Marine Corps, these are tactical-level logistics units, which typically fall under a Combat Logistics Regiment within a Marine Logistics Group.

> Note: Confusing terminology. While *Combat Logistics Platoon* (CLP) is similarly named, it's a US Army concept related to the Light Support Battalion (LSB) prototype concept. The Army doesn't currently (May 2026) seem to have any notion of a Combat Logistics Company, which is a Marine Corps concept.
