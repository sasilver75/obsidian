Altitude: ~2,000-35,786 km
Period: 2-24 hours
~2% of satellites



Sits between [[Low Earth Orbit|LEO]] and [[Geostationary Orbit|GEO]] giving a large *coverage footprint* per satellite than LEO for imaging.
Use cases:
- Almost exclusively navigation/GNSS ([[Global Navigation Satellite System]])
	- [[Global Positioning System]] (GPS; 20,200 km)
	- [[Globalnaya Navigazionnaya Sputnikovaya Sistema]] (GLONASS; Russian GPS equivalent; 19,100km)
	- [[Galileo]] (European GPS equivalent; 23,222 km)
	- [[BeiDou]] (Chinese GPS equivalent).


Satellite deployments avoid the [[Van Allen Radiation Belt]]s, which peak around 1,000-6,000 km and again at around 15,000-20,000 km.
