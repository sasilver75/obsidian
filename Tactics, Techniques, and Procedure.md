The standardized, step-by-step methods and patterns used by forces to execute missions.
- Tactics: Applied organization and movement of forces to achieve an objective
- Techniques: Methods used by an individual or unit to perform their role within a tactic
- Procedures: Standardized, detailed, and prescriptive steps taken to execute a technique

TTPs bridge the gap between high-level operational concepts and on-the-ground execution. 
- TTPS should be constantly updated through [[After-Action Review]]s, lessons learned, and joint collaboration to improve efficiency and survivability.
