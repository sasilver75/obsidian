Wavelength: ~750-900nm

Just beyond the end of visible light, humans eyes can't see it, but silicon camera sensors can. 

![[Pasted image 20260415203716.png]]
Above: I think this is a Near Infrared enhanced with the red band and green band; of course, we can't see IR 😄.

==Plants reflect NIR very strongly== (healthy leaf cell structure is highly reflective at these wavelengths), while ==water absorbs it almost completely==.
- This contrast is what makes NIR useful; it's the basis of NDVI and most vegetation indices.
