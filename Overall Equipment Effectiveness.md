A manufacturing metric combining equipment availability, performance, and quality, measuring how effectively a machine/line/process is being used compared with its full productive potential.
- ==Tells you how much of planned production time turned into good output at the intended rate.==

`OEE = Availability x Performance x Quality`
e.g.  `= 90% * 80% * 95% = 68.4%`
Where:
- Availability: Is the equipment running when it's scheduled to run?
- Performance: Is it running at expected speed?
- Quality: How much output was good product, rather than [[Scrap]] or [[Rework]]?
