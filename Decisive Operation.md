The operating that directly accomplishes a [[Commanding Officer|Commander]]'s main purpose for that phase of the mission. The action that produces the intended result, such as defeating an enemy force, seizing an objective, securing a population center, opening a route, or restoring control of an area.

See also: [[Shaping Operation]], [[Sustaining Operation]]


Example:
> [[Shaping Operation]]: Suppress enemy air defenses, disrupt reserves, and isolate the objective.
> [[Decisive Operation]]: The main force that attacks and seizes the objective.
> [[Sustaining Operation]]: Logistics, medical evacuation, maintenance, and route security that keep the force able to continue the attack.
