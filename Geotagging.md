The process of adding geographical identification metadata to various media, usually consisting of latitude and longitude coordinates, though perhaps also altitude, bearing, distance, accuracy, place name, or time stamp data.

The related term [[Geocode|Geocoding]] refers to taking non-coordinate-based geographical identifiers (e.g. a street address) and finding the associated geographic coordinates (or vice versa, via [[Geocode|Reverse Geocoding]]).
