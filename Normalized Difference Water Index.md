A remote sensing metric ==used to detect and monitor water content==, primarily surface water bodies or vegetation moisture.

For water bodies: NDWI = (Green - [[Near Infrared|NIR]])/(Green + [[Near Infrared|NIR]])   (McFeeter)
For Vegetation: NDWI = ([[Near Infrared|NIR]] - [[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]])/([[Near Infrared|NIR]] + [[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]])  (Gao, 1996)

Values range from -1 to 1.
Generally, values > 0.5 represent water bodies, while built-up areas show values 0 to 0.2.
- 0.2 to 1.0: Water surfaces
- -.1 to -.4: Green vegetation
- < 0: Bare soil, built up areas, or no moisture
