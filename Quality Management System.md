A formal set of policies, processes, procedures, records, and responsibilities an organization uses to ensure its products or services consistently meet requirements.
- ==The operating system for managing quality in an organization.==

Typically covers:
- Document control
- Training and qualifications
- Supplier management
- Inspection and testing
- Nonconformance handling
- Corrective and preventive action
- Audits
- Risk management
- Change control
- Customer requirements
- Continuous improvement
