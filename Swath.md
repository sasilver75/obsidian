The strip of the Earth's surface actually *imaged* by the sensor as satellite moves along its ground track.
- Has *width* which is determined by the sensor's FoV/pointing angle.

If the satellite is tasked [[Nadir|Off-Nadir]], the swath may not even overlap the [[Ground Track]], for instance.

The swath is composed of multiple images; the area covered by a single image acquisition on the Earth is the [[Footprint]].
