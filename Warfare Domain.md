A major environment or sphere in which military operations take place, and where forces try to gain advantage.

5 are commonly recognized:
1. Land
2. Sea
3. Air
4. Space
5. Cyberspace
Often the Electromagnetic Spectrum or Information environments are suggested or included.

As per [[FM 3-0|FM 3-0, Operations]], [[Multi-Domain Operations]] is the approach for combining effects across these domains, recognizing that each operating environment is connected to eachother.

A ground force may maneuver on land while using:
- Space for GPS/satellite comms/imagery
- Cyber to disrupt enemy networks
- Air for reconnaissance or CAS
- Fires for long-rate precision strikes
- Electromagnetic spectrum operations to jam/detect enemy systems
- Information operations to influence decision making
