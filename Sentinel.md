A satellite mission that acquires optical imagery at high spatial resolutions (10m to 60m) over land and coastal waters.

The missions Sentinel-2A and Sentinel-2B satellites were joined in orbit in 2024 by a third, Sentinel-2C, and in the future by a Sentinel-2D, which will eventually replace the A and B satellites, respectively.

Supports agricultural monitoring, emergencies management, land cover classification, and water quality.

Developed and operated by the European Space Agency.


Mission:
- Multispectral data in 13 bands in visible, near infrared, and short wave infrared.
- Global coverage of land surfaces from 56S to 84N, coastal waters, and all of hte Mediterranean sea.
- Revisiting every 10 days under the same viewing angles.
- Spatial resolution of 10m, 20m, and 60m.
- 290km field of view
- Free and open data policy
