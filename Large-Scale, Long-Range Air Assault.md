Refers to a complex, [[Division]]-level maneuver pioneered by the 101st Airborne Division (Air Assault)
- Delivers an entire [[Brigade Combat Team]] (BCT) of thousands of troops, along with their vehicles and equipment, over 500 nautical miles in a single period of darkness.
- Goal is to insert a cohesive fighting force behind enemy lines to conduct stained combat operations without immediate reinforcement.
