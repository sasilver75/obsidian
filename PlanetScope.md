The largest Earth-observation satellite constellation by satellite count, operated by [[Planet Labs]]:
- ~200+ small "Dove" cubesats in [[Low Earth Orbit|LEO]]/[[Sun-Synchronous Orbit|SSO]]
- SkySat
- SuperDove

Key specs:
- Resolution: 3-4m multispectral
- Revisits: Near-daily global coverage, which is the killer efature
- Coverage: The entire Earth's landmass every day

Good for:
- Time-series analysis where revisit matters more than resolution -- crop monitoring, deforestation alerts, construction tracking, agriculture (NDVI time series), change detection where you want daily granularity rather than Sentinel-2's 5-day revisit.

Limitations
- 3-4m resolution is coarser than Maxar; less useful for counting individual objects.
	- ==[[Planet Labs]]: Coarser but daily; [[Maxar]]: Sharper but infrequent; [[Sentinel-2]]: Free, 10m every 5 days==
- Access is subscription-based and expensive for commercial use, though Planet has a Education and Research program.
