
Usually the person or cell responsible for a specific sustainment commodity, often by [[Class of Supply]] (e.g. Class I: Food/Water or Class III: fuel/petroleum).


Distinctions:
- [[S4 Officer]]: Owns the unit's logistics requirement
	- "What do my battalions need, based on the operation?"
- [[Support Operations Officer]] (SPO): Synchronizes sustainment execution
	- "How do we support the [[Brigade]] and move resources?"
- [[Commodity Manager]]: Owns the detailed picture for one commodity
	- "What is the fuel/ammo/water/parts status, where is it, who needs it next, and how do we push it?"
