The most common type of nuclear reactor, using ordinary water (H2O) as both a neutron [[Neutron Moderator]] and coolant.
- The two main types are [[Pressurized Water Reactor|PWR]]s and [[Boiling Water Reactor|BWR]]s.

Unlike [[Heavy Water Reactor|Heavy Water]], light water is abundant but requires slightly enriched uranium fuel to maintain a chain reaction.
