A [[Remote Sensing]] Earth observation satellite program overseen by the [[Canadian Space Agency]] (CSA). 

The program has consisted of:
- RADARSAT-1 (1995-2013)
- RADARSAT-2 (2007-)
- RADARSAT Constellation (2019-)
	- This rendition uses a constellation of three small satellites to provide greater coverage while minimizing service interruptions; Revisit period is 4 days, rather than 24 days like predecessors.
