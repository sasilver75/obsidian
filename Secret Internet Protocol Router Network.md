A secure, private network used by the US [[United States Department of Defense|DoD]] and Department of State to transmit classified information up to the ==SECRET== level. 
- Operates as a "classified internet," providing services like email and web browsing while remaining entirely separated from the public internet.
- Users access it via a dedicated token (smartcard)
- Managed by the [[Defense Information Systems Agency]] (DISA)
