One of the smallest infantry tactical maneuver elements, usually made up of 4 soldiers, led by a sergeant (E-4) or corporal (E-4) and built to move/cover/fight as a small, coordinated group.

Commonly:
- Team Leader
- Rifleman
- Grenadier
- Automatic Rifleman

Fire teams are usually part of a [[Squad]], which is two fire teams plus a squad leader.
