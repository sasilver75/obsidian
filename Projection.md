==The earth is an ellipsoid with somewhat flattened poles; map projections apply transformations to convert geographic coordinates (latitude, longitude) to planar coordinates (x, y).==

Families:
- ==Conformal Projections== (Shape): ==Preserves local angles and shapes==. Good for navigation and local mapping.
	- [[Mercator|Web Mercator]], [[Universal Transverse Mercator]] (UTM).
- ==Equal-Area Projections== (Area): ==Preserves area==. Good for statistical analysis.
	- [[Albers Equal Area]]
- ==Equidistant Projections== (Distance): ==Preserves distance== from a specific point or along specific lines; good for range and coverage analysis.
	- [[Azimuthal Equidistant]]
Observe: ==No projection preserves all properties! Every projection balances different distortions; choose based on what matters for your application.==
