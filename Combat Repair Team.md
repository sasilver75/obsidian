In the [[Light Support Battalion]] (LSB) prototype concept, Combat Repair Teams are a forward maintenance element from the LSB's maintenance organization that is aligned with or sent to a maneuver battalion to provide field maintenance, recovery, diagnostics, and urgent repair support. The forward "fix and recover equipment" element.

Responsible for:
- Vehicle/equipment troubleshooting
- Field-level repairs
- Battle damage assessment and repair
- Recovery coordination or recovery support
- Repair parts coordination
- Keeping combat platforms operational and close to the fight


In the old [[Forward Support Company]] model:
- One FSC habitually supports a [[Battalion]] with distribution, maintenance, and feeding.

In the new prototype [[Light Support Battalion]] model:
- [[Combat Logistics Platoon]]s forward distribution
- [[Combat Repair Team]]s forward repair/maintenance
- The [[Light Support Battalion]] + [[Support Operations Officer]] centrally tasks and reallocates them by mission priority.
