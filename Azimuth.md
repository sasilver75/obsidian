==Elevation and Azimuth are coordinates used to pinpoint an object's location in the sky RELATIVE to an observer.==
- Azimuth: The compass direction (horizontal), measuring 0-360 degrees clockwise from north.
- Elevation (or altitude): The vertical angle upward from the horizon (0) to the object (90 at zenith)
