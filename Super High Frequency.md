==CONFUSING TERMINOLOGY NOTE==: Terminology was coined in the early radio era when anything about 300MHz was genuinely exotic. At the time, UHF was the frontier. The names predate radar entirely and were never revised as frequencies pushed into the tens and hundreds of GHz.
