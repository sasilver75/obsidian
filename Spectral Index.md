Any mathematical combination of spectral bands designed to highlight a specific surface property.
- A subset of spectral indexes are [[Vegetation Index]]es.


| TYPE       | EXAMPLE                                            | WHAT IT DETECTS          |
| ---------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Vegetation | [[Normalized Difference Vegetation Index\|NDVI]]   | Plant biomass/health     |
| Water      | [[Normalized Difference Water Index\|NDWI]], MNDWI | Surface water, moisture  |
| Burn       | [[Normalized Burn Ratio\|NBR]], BAI                | Fire scars               |
| Built-up   | [[Normalized Difference Built-up Index\|NDBI]], UI | Urban surfaces           |
| Soil       | BSI, SOCI                                          | Bre soil, organic carbon |
