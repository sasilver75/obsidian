  - Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer

Satellite-based sensor used for earth and climate measurements.
There are two in orbit:
- One on the Terra satellite (EOS AM; NASA 1999)
- One on the Aqua satellite (EOS PM; 2002)

Since 2011, MODIS operations have been supplemented by VIIRS sensors, like the one aboard Suomi NPP.
These systems often conduct similar operations due to similar designs and orbits, though they have subtle differences.

The majority of bands have a spatial resolution of 1km; each pixel represents a 1km x 1km area on the gound. Also includes bands with spatial resolutions of 250 m or 500 m.
