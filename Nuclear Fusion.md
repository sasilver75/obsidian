> "I have a PhD in Fusion. When someone tells me that we're going to have Fusion on the grid in 203X... well, no one is really even thinking about issues like how to do maintenance, and until you do that, you don't have anything economical. fission averages a 93% uptimes, which is remarkable, but they weren't birthed out of the womb with these rates, they were birthed with 50-60% uptimes, and it's through DECADES of learning that it became 93% uptime. Startups promise that they will be 93%, and I'm like no way! There will be a lot of challenges, and learning has to happen. Don't sell people on something you can't deliver, be honest that it will take to the second, third, fourth time to deliver reliability and low maintenance cost that you need to be cost-effective."
> [Jeff Waksman](https://youtu.be/9Ij8z5WZbEg), program manager of Project Pele
