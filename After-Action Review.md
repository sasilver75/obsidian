---
aliases:
  - AAR
---
A structured debrief conducted after a mission, operation, exercise, or training event to identify what happened, why it happened, and how performance can be improved.

Typically asks four core questions:
1. What was supposed to happen?
2. What actually happened?
3. Why were there differences?
4. What should we sustain or improve next time?

An AAR is not meant to assign blame, its purpose is learning: capturing lessons, improving tactics/procedures, reinforcing effective actions, and correlating weaknesses before the next operation or training iteration.
