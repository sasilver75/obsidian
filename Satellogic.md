An [[Remote Sensing|Earth Observation]] company focused on making high-resolution satellite imagery affordable and accessible. 
- Compared to other commercial EO companies like [[BlackSky]], [[Maxar]], [[Planet Labs|Planet]], Satellogic leads more towards affordable high-resolution and hyperspectral imaging, disrupting on price.

Key aspects:
1. Satellites: Operates a constellation of small satellites capable of sub-meter resolution in multispectral and hyperspectral imaging.
2. Business Model: Differentiates on cost, aiming to commoditize satellite imagery by offering low-cost, high-volume data
3. Hyperspectral: Notable for including [[Hyperspectral]] sensors (hundreds of spectral bands) on small satellites, which is unusual at their price point.
4. Revisit: Designed to remap the entire Earth frequently
5. Origin: Founded in Argentina in 2010
6. Customers: Mix of government and commercial, with a notable contract with the US government, also partnered with VisionAT and others
7. SPACE listing: Went public via SPAC in 2022.
