Oceans and atmosphere agency.
- *"NOAA owns the sky, the coast, and the sea; weather, water, and the datums everything else is measured against.*

In geospatial:
- Weather satellites ([[GOES-R]], JPSS)
- Coastla LiDAR: repeat surveys of US coastlines
- Bathymetry: Ocean depth data, nautical charts
- Tidal datum/information (what does "sea level" really mean?)
- Climate data archives (historical weather stations, sea surface temperatures)
