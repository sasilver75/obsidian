A small tactical maneuver element made up of ~9 soldiers, composed of 2 [[Fire Team]]s and a leader, usually a staff sergeant (E-6).

The level above a [[Fire Team]] and below a [[Platoon]].


Two common types:
- ==Rifle Squad==: The main maneuver elements of a platoon, closing with and destroying the enemy.
- ==Weapons Squad==: Heavier supporting fire for a a platoon, helps suppress/destroy enemy positions so that the rifle squads can move.
