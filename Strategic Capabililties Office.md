A US [[United States Department of Defense|DoD]] office established in 2012 by Ash Carter in the Obama Administration, rapidly develops and prototypes innovative, often unconventional, applications for existing military technology to create strategic, unexpected within 3-5 years. 

> We view ourselves as the Q branch of the DoD. The vast majority of what SCO does, I can't talk about. It's a deep irony that by far the most public program we've done is the nuclear reactor, because moving nuclear reactors around the country... you can't actually sneak them around, which forces us to be very public.
> - Jeff Waksman
