An essential military status report used by Army units to forecast sustainment requirements, coordinate resupplies, and track combat readiness.

Provides a snapshot of critical resources like ammunition, fuel, and medical supplies to help commanders make informed decisions.

> Deciding which unit requires the next day of supply can be a significant event. This planning is achieved through ==requirements forecasting== and is enabled through ==daily logistics status reports== ([[Logistics Status|LOGSTAT]]s) provided by maneuver units.

> At the National Training Center, [[Army Echelons|Brigade Combat Team]]s average a [[Logistics Status|LOGSTAT]] turn-in rate of ==76 percent==, and these reports are often ==late== and ==inaccurate==.

> Complicating logistics planning further is Multi-Domain Battle. In this environment, joint forces work together using cross-domain capabilities to deter aggression not only in air/land but also sea/space/cyberspace. These operations require logisticians to support the battle across multiple domains.
