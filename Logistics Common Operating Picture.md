A centralized, real-time dashboard that integrates supply chain, inventory, and asset data into a single source of truth.
- Provides commanders and managers immediate visibility over resources, enabling faster decision-making, predictive logistics, and seamless synchronization between field operations and central command.


![[Pasted image 20260515212343.png]]
Show you at one glance: How are we doing? How are the units doing?
- The color coding is standard in defense logistics; you can see... [[Class of Supply]]s here. Green means good, Yellow (Amber?)... you can set thresholds (e.g. 60%). Red is below that, and Black is you're in trouble.
- The unit has what's called Days of Supplies for roughly 3 days... this is typically the rhythm how supplies are planned... and you want to make sure that with the current supplies, they can sustain themselves for three days.
