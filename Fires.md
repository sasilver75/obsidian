A [[Warfighting Function]]

The related tasks and systems that provide collective and coordinated use of Army indirect fires, air and missile defense, and joint fires through the targeting process.

In other words, ==the use of weapon systems to create a specific lethal or nonlethal effect on target.==
- Traditionally focused on fire support systems like artillery and close air support (CAS), but increasingly refers to non-lethal systems like information operations, cyberwarfare.

The Marine Corps defines this as:
> "Fires harass, suppress, neutralize, or destroy to accomplish the targeting objective, which may be to disrupt, delay, limit, persuade, or influence."
