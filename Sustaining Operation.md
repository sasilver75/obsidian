An operation that enables shaping and decisive operations by maintaining the force's ability to continue operating.

Includes the activities needed to preserve combat power, extend endurance, and keep units supplied, protected, moved, repaired, and medically supported.

See also: [[Shaping Operation]], [[Decisive Operation]]


Example:
> [[Shaping Operation]]: Suppress enemy air defenses, disrupt reserves, and isolate the objective.
> [[Decisive Operation]]: The main force that attacks and seizes the objective.
> [[Sustaining Operation]]: Logistics, medical evacuation, maintenance, and route security that keep the force able to continue the attack.
