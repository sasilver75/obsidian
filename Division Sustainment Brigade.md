Directly supports their assigned [[Army Echelons|Division]]

Consists of:
- A special troops battalion
- A division sustainment support battalion ([[Division Sustainment Support Battalion|DSSB]])
- Can also control 5 battalions attached from higher up.

The Special Troops Battalion controls controls attached companies, particularly in the realm of human resources, finance, field feeding/food service, and mortuary affairs.



The DSSB meanwhile is a logistic battalion that... are replacing Combat Sustainment Support Battalions (CSSBs) at the division level. The former is more directly aligned to needs of Division, while the latter are more modular and held at higher levels or attached to divisions. Before the mid-2010s, sustainment brigades would support divisions, but weren't under their commands, and did not necessarily have the right company mix optimized for supporting a division as opposed to a corps.
![[Pasted image 20260517222839.png]]


DSSBs doctrinally include an HQ and HQ company, Composite supply company, composite truck company, and a support maintenance company. Can also control up to 4 additional attached companies.
