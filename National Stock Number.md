A standardized 13-digit number that's used to identify an item of supply in government and military logistics
- NSNs help different agencies and military services order, track, store, and manage the same item consistently, even if vendors use different part numbers or names.

Has two parts:
- ==Federal Supply Class== (FSC): First 4 digits, indicating general category of item
- ==National Item Identification Number== (NIIN): Last 9 digits, identifying the specific item

Example:
```
1234-00-123-4567

1234 = Federal Supply Class
00-123-4567 = NIIN
```
