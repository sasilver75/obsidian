An AI-powered defense platform developed by [[Palantir]] that integrates, analyzes, and acts on massive volumes of sensor and intelligence data for the U.S. military, serving as a core "command and control" ([[Command and Control|C2]]) system.

Leverages computer vision and AI to identify targets and recommend actions, compressing the [[OODA Loop]] for military operators.
