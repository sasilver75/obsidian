In December 2024, Palantir announced a four-year Army Vantage/Army Data Platform agreement valued at $400-600M... in 2025, this and other Palantir contracts were consolidated into a broader 10-year enterprise agreement capped at up to $10B.

The US Army's enterprise data analytics and decision platform, powered by Palantir software.
- Pulls data from lots of Army systems into one environment so that commanders, staff, analysts, and soldiers can see readiness, personnel, logistics, finance, recruiting, risk, installation, and other operational data in one place.
- Supports dashboards, trend analysis, AI workflows, predictive analytics, collaboration, role-based access control.
