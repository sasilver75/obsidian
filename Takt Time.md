---

In manufacturing, the pace at which a product must be completed to meet customer demand.
- Used in [[Lean Manufacturing]] to align production flow with demand, balance work across stations, identify bottlenecks, and avoid overproduction.

`Takt Time = Available Production Time / Customer Demand`
- If you have 480 minutes per shift and customers demand 240 units per shift, the takt time is 2 minutes per unit.
