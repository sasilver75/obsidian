A low-power, license-free radio [[Modulation]] technology.
It allows you to transmit small packets of data (telemetry, text messages) over massive distances (up to 5 to 15 miles) using very little power.

"""
- Modulation: Uses [[Chirp Spread Spectrum]] (CSS), which spreads a narrow signal over a wider band. this makes it highly resistant to interference and allows receivers to detect signals "below the noise floor."
- Frequencies: Operates on sub-GHz ISM bands, as well as newer 2.4 GHz band for higher bandwidth.
- While LoRA is just the physical radio layer, protocols like LoRaWAN manage network architecture for the IoT.
"""


[[Meshtastic]] is a popular application, an open-source mesh-networking project allowing you to send end-to-end encrypted text messages off-grid, without cellular or internet networks. Messages hop from radio to radio to extend coverage.
