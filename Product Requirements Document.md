A document that answers ==*What are we building, for whom, and why.*==
- Owned by ***product***
- Audience: engineers, design, leadership, sometimes sales and support.
- Frames the problem and the user: who has the pain, what are they trying to do, and what does success look like?
- Includes ==user stories==, ==scope== (in/out), ==success metrics==, ==constraints==, ==rough timeline==.
- Deliberately under-specifies *how,* leaving room for design and engineering to make implementation calls.
- Lives at the "feature" or "release" altitude.

Typical sections: Problem statement, goals/non-goals, user stories, requirements, success metrics, open questions, out-of-scope.

# [[Specification|Spec]]/[[Specification|Design Doc]] vs [[Product Requirements Document]]
- Specification/Design Doc: *How will we build it?*
- Product Requirements Doc: *What are we building, for whom, and why?*
