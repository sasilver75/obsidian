==Cold Criticality==/==Zero-Power Criticality==: A foundational milestone where a reactor achieves a self-sustaining chain reaction at low temperature without generating significant heat or power. 
- This validates core design, fuel performance, and control systems, serving as a "first heartbeat" for new reactor designs. 
- Validates [[Neutronics]], fuel geometry, and [[Moderator]]s (e.g. graphite) without the risks of high temperature operations.
