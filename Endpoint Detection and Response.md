A security category that monitors ==endpoints== (laptops, desktops, servers) for malicious activity, records behavioral data, and gives security teams the tools to investigate and respond. 
- The word "endpoint" just means "any individual device that runs code" as opposed to network gear in between.

It's the modern evolution of "Antivirus"; while AV asked "is this file a known bad fale," EDR asks "is what's happening on this machine behaviorally suspicious?"
