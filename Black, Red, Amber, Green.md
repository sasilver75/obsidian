In the Army, the BRAG system is used to measure supply levels, equipment maintenance, and overall mission readiness. 

Generally:
- Green: Fully mission-capable with 90% or more of required supplies/personnel/equipment.
- Amber: Partially capable, but can still operate. 70-89%
- Red: Significantly degraded, needs support to execute missions.  < 70%
- Black: Non-mission capable due to severe shortages or catastrophic equipment failure.
