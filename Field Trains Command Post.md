The command/control node for the [[Battalion]]'s [[Brigade Support Area]].
- Its job is to coordinate the battalion's *rear sustainment work*, whereas the [[Combat Trains Command Post]] is the Battalion's *forward sustainment node* closer to the fight.


# Comparison (Closest-to-[[Front Line of Troops|FLOT]], descending)
- [[Company Trains]]
	- Closest to [[Company]], main purpose is immediate company sustainment.
	- Usually the Company 1SG runs the trains day to day, company [[Executive Officer|XO]] might coordinate resupply, maintenance, [[Logistics Package|LOGPAC]]s. Coordinates with the [[Combat Trains Command Post|CTCP]], the Battalion [[S4 Officer|S4]] officer, and the supporting [[Forward Support Company|FSC]].
- [[Combat Trains Command Post]] (CTCP)
	- Forward [[Battalion]] sustainment node near the fight.
	- Doctrinally led by the Battalion [[S4 Officer]].
	- Coordinates with the Battalion main [[Tactical Operations Center|TOC]], [[Company Trains]], the [[Field Trains Command Post]], and the [[Brigade Support Battalion]] (BSB)
- [[Field Trains Command Post]] (FTCP)
	- Runs the [[Battalion]]'s rear sustainment area and manages [[Field Trains Command Post|Field Trains]]. The logistics and administrative node kept further back than the CTCP, for less immediate but still essential support needs.
	- Often run by the FSC XO, Assistant S4, or a designated OIC/NCOIC.  
	- Coordinates heavily with the [[Brigade Support Battalion|BSB]] [[Commodity Manager]]s, the [[Brigade Support Area|BSA]], [[Support Operations Officer|SPO]] section, and the forward [[Combat Trains Command Post|CTCP]].
- [[Brigade Support Area]] (BSA)
	- [[Brigade]]-level rear area, larger sustainment hub.  The [[S4 Officer]] and [[Support Operations Officer|SPO]] help run operations, movement, defense, and support distribution.
