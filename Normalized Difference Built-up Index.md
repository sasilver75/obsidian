A geospatial index used to map and monitor built-up areas (buildings, roads, impervious surfaces) by leveraging high reflectivity of Shortwave Infrared ([[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]]) and low reflection of Near-Infrared ([[Near Infrared|NIR]]).

Calculated as ([[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]] - [[Near Infrared|NIR]])/([[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]] + [[Near Infrared|NIR]]), with a range from -1 to 1
- Positive values indicate built-up areas (buildings, concrete surfaces, asphalt, roads)
- Negative values indicate non-built up areas, typically vegetation (e.g. forests, crops)
