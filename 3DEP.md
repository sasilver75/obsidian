[[United States Geological Survey|USGS]]'s 3D Elevation Program, an ongoing ==effort to collect high-quality LiDAR coverage of the entire contiguous US==.

Large portions are now covered, with the data freely available via the USGS National Map and [[OpenTopography]].
- Point clouds available as [[LAZ]] and [[Entwine Point Tiles|EPT]] files.
- Derived [[Digital Elevation Model|DEM]]s in 1m and 1/3 arc-seconds resolution are also available.
- This is a ==genuinely transformative public dataset, and the kind of thing that would cost hundreds of millions to acquire commercially.==
