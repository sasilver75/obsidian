The ==K-25 Plant== in [[Oak Ridge National Laboratory|Oak Ridge]], Tennessee was a massive Manhattan Project facility built in 1943-1945 to enrich uranium for atomic bombs via gaseous diffusion. The largest building in the world at the time, demolished in 2013.

K-25, Y-12, X-10, these are all locations in a grid system. 

X-10 is now called [[Oak Ridge National Laboratory]], and is the location where, after [[Enrico Fermi]] proved out the first artificial nuclear reactor, [[Chicago Pile Reactor]], they wanted to make one that ran continuously.
