Defense/intelligence agency
- *"NGA is the intelligence community's map-maker; they own WGS84 and the classified view of Earth's surface.*

Products
- Produces classified and unclassified geospatial intelligent (GEINT)
- Maintains DTED (Digital Terrain Elevation Data) - Global elevation
- Produces NTM (National Technical Means): classified satellite imagery
- Manages [[WGS84]]
- Publishes many unclassified products (world port index, sailing directions, aeronautical charts)
- Funds a lot of foundational geospatial research and standards
