A remote sensing index ==used to identify burned areas and quantify fire severity==.

It compares high near-infrared ([[Near Infrared|NIR]]) reflectance from healthy vegetation with high shortwave-infrared ([[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]]) reflection from scorched areas.

Calculated as ([[Near Infrared|NIR]] - [[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]])/([[Near Infrared|NIR]] + [[Short-Wave Infrared|SWIR]]), with a -1 to 1 range.

High values indicate  (e.g. 0.1 to 1) indicate healthy vegetation, while lower or negative values indicate recently burned areas, bare soil, or low vegetation.
