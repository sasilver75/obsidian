Company behind  [[DuckDB]], [[DuckLake]], founded by Hannes Muhleisen and Mark Raasveldt during their research at CWI, the dutch national research institute, in 2021.

DuckDB itself is [[MIT License]]d and will always be, but they offer DuckDB Cloud, a managed cloud service for running DuckDB at scale, along with enterprise support contracts, consulting and professional services.

DuckDB has had an outszed impact on the industry, relative to the size of the team; in a geospatial context, DuckDB's geospatial exxtension has made [[GeoParquet]] practically useful for ad-hoc geospatial analysis without having to need [[PostGIS]].
