A 2.5-ton 4x4 payload class truck used for unit mobility, personnel transport, and light supply re-supply.
- (Note: 2.5 ton means that it's designed to carry 5,000 pounds of cargo, not that it weighs 2.5 tons. Commonly called "deuce and a half")

The standard cargo variant can carry 5,000 pounds on its bed and is commonly found with bench seats for soldiers.
