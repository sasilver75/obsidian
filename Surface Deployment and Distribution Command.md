The U.S. Army command that manages much of the military's surface transportation and distribution, especially movement by ship, rail, truck, and port operations.

Here's a way of thinking of it:
[[Defense Logistics Agency|DLA]] : [[Army Materiel Command]] (AMC) :: [[US Transportation Command|TRANSCOM]] : [[Surface Deployment and Distribution Command]]
Just like AMC is the Army-specific *stuff* command, SDDC is the Army-specific *transport* command, in comparison to their joint counterparts (DLA, TRANSCOM)
