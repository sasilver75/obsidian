The [[United States Department of Defense|DoD]]'s agency that supplies common commodities such as fuel, food, medical materiel, construction supplies, uniforms, and repair parts across the joint force.

See also: [[Army Materiel Command]], US [[US Transportation Command]] (USTRANSCOM)


c.f. [[US Transportation Command|TRANSCOM]]
- DLA is about *stuff*: sourcing, managing, stocking, and supplying common materiel.
- TRANSCOM is about *movement*: transporting people and cargo globally.
