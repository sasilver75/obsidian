
A ==one-to-many== routing method, where data is sent from a single source to a specific group of devices that have opted in (subscribed) to receive it.

Devices join a specific multicast group address. The sender transmits the data packet once, and the network duplicates it only for the subscribed devices.  This is much more efficient than using [[Unicast]] to thousands of people or using [[Broadcast]] (interrupting everyone).

Common uses are live video streaming, IPTV broadcasts, video conferencing, and online gaming.
