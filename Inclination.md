An [[Orbital Elements|Orbital Element]], part of a set of parameters describing the orbit of an object around a central body.

In [[Satellite]] parlance, the angle between the satellite's orbital plane and Earth's equatorial plane.

![[Pasted image 20260527121723.png]]


0 degree inclination: [[Equatorial Orbit]]
28.5 degree inclination: Satellite [[Ground Track]]s can pass as far north and south as +/- 28.5 latitude.
