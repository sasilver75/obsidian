Company that launched around 2019 as a ==[[Tile]] hosting service==, explicitly targeting the open-source mapping community:
- People using [[MapLibre]], [[Leaflet]], and [[OpenLayers]] who didn't want [[Mapbox]] dependency.

They host ==tiles based on [[OpenStreetMap]] data==, and offer several styles, including popular ports of open source styles.
- Because it's based on OSM data, you get the full richness of community-contributed geographic data.

Their `Alidade Smooth Dark` style is widely considered the ==best freely-available dark map style,== designed by Stamen Design (famous data visualization studio), and Stadia now hosts and maintains it.
- Warm dark tone, rather than cold blue-black, subtle street hierarchy, and clean typography.

Runs on ==a global [[Content Delivery Network|CDN]] infrastructure==, so performance is generally good, and comparable to Mapbox for most use cases.

Pricing
- Free tier is 300k requests/month with no CC required
- Paid plans start at $20/mo for higher volume

Tradeoffs:
- +: No credit card for free tier, most generous free tier of the commercial options.
- +: Alidade Smooth Dark is genuinely excellent for data viz.
- +: Explicitly built for [[MapLibre]]; good documentation, no friction.
- +: Small, responsive company.
- -: Less name recognition than Mapbox
