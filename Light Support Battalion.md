A US Army [[Sustainment]] [[Battalion]] that's designed to support a light or mobile brigade with more dispersed, mobile and survivable logistics than the traditional centralized [[Brigade Support Battalion]] model (e.g. [[Brigade Support Area|BSA]]).

Provides Supply distribution, Fuel and water, Ammunition support, Field maintenance and recovery, Medical support, Field feeding, Logistics planning and control.

In the Army's recent transformation efforts, some [[Brigade Support Battalion]] (BSBs) are being converted into [[Light Support Battalion]]s (LSBs) to support new formations like [[Brigade Combat Team|Mobile Brigade Combat Team]]s (MBCTs) in [[Large Scale Combat Operations]], where traditional centralized [[Brigade Support Area|BSA]]s would be vulnerable to detection and long-range precision fire.

The idea is to move away from one large, vulnerable brigade support area, and towards smaller, distributed logistics nodes. 
- It uses [[Light Support Area]]s (LSAs), ==made of several [[Light Logistics Cluster]]s (LLCs).==


Old Model: One [[Brigade Support Area]]
New Model: One [[Light Support Area]] made up of multiple [[Light Logistics Cluster]]s

The LSA can displace more flexibly because the entire sustainment system doesn't have to move at once.


Traditional Model:
- [[Brigade Support Battalion]] operates [[Brigade Support Area]]s
- [[Forward Support Company|Forward Support Companies]] support specific maneuver [[Battalion]]s

New (Prototype) Model:
- [[Light Support Battalion]] operates a [[Light Support Area]]
- [[Light Logistics Cluster]]s operate within the [[Light Support Area]]
- Combat Logistics [[Platoon]]/Combat Repair Teams provide tailored support to task forces.

So the [[Light Support Battalion]] most directly replaces/transforms the [[Brigade Support Battalion]], replacing the centralized [[Brigade Support Area|BSA]] model with a [[Logistics Support Area|LSA]]/[[Light Logistics Cluster|LLC]]s.

The [[Light Support Battalion]] also changes what happens to [[Forward Support Company]]s, though. In the 225th LSB prototype, some FSCs were deactivate or consolidated, pulled into the LSB and reorganized into things like:
- [[General Support Company]] (GSC)
- [[Combat Logistics Platoon]]s (CLPs)
- [[Combat Repair Team]]s (CRTs)
- Centralized field feeding
- Centralized field maintenance
- More planning authority in the [[Support Operations Officer]] (SPO) section

The big operational shift is from *habitual FSC support to each maneuver battalion* towards *centralized sustainment planning with task-organized forward support packages.*

So the [[Light Support Battalion|LSB]] commander and the [[Support Operations Officer|SPO]] can reallocate logistics capacity across the brigade based on the current fight, instead of every maneuver battalion having a relatively fixed FSC slice.
