
A map is an abstraction that represents the world as discrete, labeled [[Feature]]s (vectors, symbols, text) rather than a continuous grid of pixel values.

There are maps that are about as old as the oldest human language, they're an intrinsic part of what it means to be human!
# Maps vs Imagery
- ==Imagery== is simply raw or processed sensor data. A pixel has a reflectance value. It shows you what the ground looks like.
