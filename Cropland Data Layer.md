A [[United States Department of Agriculture|USDA]] National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS)-produced annual product, produced since 1997.
- Maps ==What crop is growing in each field across the US,== updated annually.
- Produced by analyzing [[Landsat]] and [[Sentinel|Sentinel-2]] imagery, combined with ground surveys.
	- Each pixel is labeled with a specific crop type (corn, soybeans, wheat, cotton, etc.) - 100+ crop categories.
	- US-only coverage, 30 resolution, published each year AFTER the growing season.

Freely available and used widely in agricultural ML research.
