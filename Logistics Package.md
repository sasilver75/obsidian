A U.S. Army resupply operation used to deliver essential supplies, such as fuel, ammunition, and rations, to forward-deployed units.
- A critical component of sustaining combat operations, typically involving organized convoy movements from a brigade support area to forward operating locatinos.




> "5 Gallon jugs are great, but how long does it take to fill up canteens off that? Some units are getting 2QT canteens to they can do quick hot-swaps... Being able to trade out 100oz camelback bladders... and maintain momentum. The longer you're at a 5 gallon jug, a [[Water Buffalo]], a [[HIPPO]], the bigger target you become. On the flip side: smaller containers require longer amount of time to build the packages. We're trading the time it takes to run missions for the time it takes to refill the commodity."
> - JRTC Crucible Podcast
