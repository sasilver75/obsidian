[[Spectral Index]]es that specifically target photosynthetic activity, biomass, canopy structure, or plant stress. 
- Often takes advantage of chlorophyll's absorption in red and strong reflectance in [[Near Infrared|NIR]].

Common examples:
- [[Normalized Difference Vegetation Index]] (NDVI)
- EVI: Corrects for soil/atmosphere, better in dense canopy
- SAVI: NDVI-adjusted for sparse vegetation over bright soil
- NDRE: Uses red edge band, better for crop stress detection
- LAI: Leaf area index, more physically grounded
