A ==catalog/directory of publicly available datasets== (broad scope) hosted on [[Amazon S3|S3]].
- Broad scope, ==not just geospatial -- genomics, climate, satellite imagery, census data, etc.==
- Tells you *what exists and where it lives* (which public S3 bucket)
- ==Static metadata pages, not a queryable API==
- Think of it as a phonebook.
