pron. "Crisp"; often called a CRSP yard.

A specialized logistics hub that serves as the central location for receiving, staging, and processing all incoming and outgoing cargo, equipment, and supplies.

Acts as the military equivalent of a massive post office or sorting center. Key operations include:
- Cargo Management: Unloading/tracking/staging vehicles, containers, supplies.
- In-Transit Visibility: Tracking exactly where equipment is and ensuring it reaches the correct unit.
- Distribution: Processing materials/goods/equipment so they can be forwarded to troops or redeployed elsewhere.
