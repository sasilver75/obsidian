
In the US Army, a squadron is a [[Battalion]]-equivalent-level unit used mainly in Cavalry organizations, usually commanded by a lieutenant colonel (O-5) with a CSM (E-9) senior enlisted advisor.

Its subordinate units are typically [[Troop]]s, with the higher unit typically being a [[Brigade]] or [[Regiment]].

Typical role is reconnaissance, security, cavalry, aviation, or special operations support.

> "A cavalry-style battalion, with troops instead of companies."
