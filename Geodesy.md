aliases:
  - Geodetics
---
The science of measuring and representing the geometry, gravity, and spatial orientation in of the Earth in temporally varying 3D space.
- It's called "planetary geodesy" when studying other astronomical bodies, like planets or circumplanetary systems.

Provides the scientific basis for mapping, navigation, and positioning, and supports applications like infrastructure development (incl. construction), natural resource management, mineral exploration, and geophysics.

Its measurements underpin modern geospatial reference frames used in transportation, satellite systems, global trade, and timekeeping.
