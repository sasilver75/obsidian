Any detectable footprint, such as heat, sound, or electronic signals, that an asset or soldier emits, making them vulnerable to tracking and targeting.

Signature Management techniques like Camouflage, Concealment, adjusting flight profiles (e.g. Nap-of-the-Earth, Terrain Masking) is critical to avoiding this.
