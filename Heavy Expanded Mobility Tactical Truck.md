The HEMTT is an 8x8 heavy-duty diesel tactical vehicle that is the backbone of Army logistics and transport since 1982.

Known for its rugged off-road mobility and massive payload capacity, the truck is frequently adapted into several specialized variants:
- **M977** - Standard cargo carrier, often equipped with a material handling crane.
- **M978** - Tanker variant used to transport 2,500 gallons of fuel.
- **M983** - Tractor configuration used for towing heavy loads, including Patriot missile systems.
- **M984** - Heavy wrecker for recovering disabled or stuck military vehicles.
- **M1120** - Features a hydraulic Load Handling System (LHS) for picking up and dropping off flatracks and container without the need for a crane to load/unload.
