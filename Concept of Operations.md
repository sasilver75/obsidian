A formal, high-level document or graphic defining *==how a military force commander plans to employ, organize, and support capabilities to achieve specific objectives over the near term==* (1-7 years(
- Outlines the WHAT and WHY of a mission or system, bridging operational needs with technical requirements.

(A military version of something that is a blend of a [[Product Requirements Document|PRD]] and [[Specification|Technical Design Doc]])
