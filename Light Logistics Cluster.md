One of the smaller sustainment nodes inside a [[Light Support Area]], which is maintained by a [[Light Support Battalion]].

LLC Blue: Rear cluster for the whole LSA, functioning somewhat like the old field-trains/rear-support node.
- 72 hour field maintenance
- Supply support activity
- Food ration break point
- Bulk water
- Bulk fuel

LLC Red and LLC White:
- Forward multi-class distribution clusters.
- Their role is to push support closer to task forces, similar to a forward logistics element or "mini-BSA" package.
- Capabilities
	- Bulk water distribution
	- Fuel distribution
	- Ammunition distribution
	- Split Role 2 medical capability

### How the Flow works
- [[Division Sustainment Support Battalion]] resupplies ==[[Light Logistics Cluster|LLC]] Blue==
- LLC Blue redistributes to ==LLC Red== and ==LLC White==
- Task forces conduct [[Logistics Package|LOGPAC]]s with LLC Red/White
- Combat logistics platoons push support further forward

So LLC Blue is the rear receiver and redistributor, while LLC Red/White are forward distribution clusters.

A Classic BSA concentrates too much sustainment in one place, making it easier to detect/attack/disrupt/force to displace. The LLC model spreads capability across multiple smaller nodes.

If LLC Red is attacked or forced to move, LLC white can pick up missions. If one cluster stops scheduled LOGPACs, another cluster can become the logistics node temporarily.

==The model gains survivability, redundancy, and mobility, but costs more in coordination.==
- The weak point is ==forecasting== and ==synchronization==. Without a [[Forward Support Company|FSC]] commander embedded with each supported unit, the TF [[S4 Officer]] and [[Support Operations Officer|SPO]] must understand requirements accurately and keep the sustainment picture current.

>In the LBCT sustainment model, the LSB runs a distributed LSA made of multiple LLCs; LLC Blue acts as the rear sustainment cluster, while LLC Red/White act as forward multi-class distribution clusters that sustain task forces and provide ==redundancy== if one cluster is attacked or displaced.
