MW: Megawatt: An instantaneous rate of power production or consumption ("power speed")
MWh: Megawatt Hour: The total amount of energy generated or used over time ("energy volume")

MWt: Megawatts Thermal: The power produced by the nuclear reactor in the form of heat, representing the total fission power output.
MWe: Megawatts Electric: The electricity exported to the grid, typically maintaining a ratio of 3 MWt to 1MWe due to ~33% efficiency.
