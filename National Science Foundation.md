An independent US federal agency supporting science and engineering in all 50 states and US territories.

They don't collect data themselves, but fund the science that produces it:
- Funds university remote sensing and GIS research
- Funds [[OpenTopography]], the LiDAR/DEM portal built on NSF money
- Funds Arctic/Antarctic research
- Funds development of open-source geospatial tools
- NCAR ([[National Center for Atmospheric Research]]) is NSF-funded
