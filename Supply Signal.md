An informal, non-doctrinal [[Gallatin AI]] term for any observation that updates the current or projected sustainment demand.

A [[Logistics Status|LOGSTAT]] is a specific report/workflow, whereas a Supply Signal is broader; any input that changes the system's understanding of supply state.

> "The best LOGSTAT is no LOGSTAT, meaning it's automatically sensed."

> "Re: supply signals, you want to make it as easy as possible for the warfighter not to have to enter LOGSTATs manually in a keyboard."

Examples:
- Fuel sensor reports a tank level 
- Radio communication: "[[Class of Supply|Class III]] amber, black in two hours."
