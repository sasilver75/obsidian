The total amount of electromagnetic radiation (light, height, radio waves) emitted, transmitted, or received per unit time.
- Used to describe the "total power" of EM radiation, often used to describe the output of a source like a lamp or the sun, regardless of direction.

It measures the rate of energy transfer, with the SI unit being the [[Power|Watt]] (W), which equals one [[Joule]] per second (J/s).
