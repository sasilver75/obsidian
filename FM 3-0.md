The Army's capstone doctrinal manual for military operations, outlining how the Army forces contribute landpower to the joint force and integrate air/land/sea/space/cyber capabilities to achieve military objectives and win conflicts.

FM 3-0
- Codifies [[Multi-Domain Operations]] (MDO), focusing heavily on [[Large Scale Combat Operations]] (LSCO) against peer adversaries.
- A professional guide for commanders, staffs, and leaders of theater armies, corps, divisions, and brigades.
- Rests on the combined application of Army and joint capabilities, with imperatives including constant observation, convergence of forces, and dominating the [[Electromagnetic Spectrum]].
