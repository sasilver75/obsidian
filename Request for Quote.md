In manufacturing, a formal request sent to supplier asking them to provide pricing and terms for specific goods or services. 

An RFQ is usually used when the buyer already knows what they want, and needs suppliers to quote cost/delivery/availability/commercial terms.
- =="How much will you charge, and under what terms, for this specific thing?"==

Typically includes:
- Item or service description
- Quantity
- Technical requirements or specifications
- Drawings, part numbers, or standards
- Required delivery date
- Shipping destination
- Packaging or labeling requirements
- Quality Requirements
- Payment terms
- Quote due date

Supplier responses usually inclucde:
- Unit price
- Total price
- Lead time
- [[Minimum Order Quantity]] (MOQ)
- Validity period
- Exceptions or assumptions
- Freight or shipping terms
- Payment terms
