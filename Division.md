
A division is a large tactical formation made up of multiple [[Brigade]]s, usually commanded by a major general. Typically 10,000 to 20,00 soldiers, depending on mission and attachments. Its role is to command and integrate multiple brigades in major operations.

A Division's higher headquarters is usually a  [[Corps]].
