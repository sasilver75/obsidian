An officer that leads operational/combat units.
- An infantry captain commanding a Company
- An armor lieutenant colonel commanding a Battalion
- A naval warfare officer commanding a ship

Compare with:
- [[Staff Officer]]: Intelligence officer, logistics officer, operational planner, personnel officer.
	- Includes both [[S4 Officer]]s and [[Support Operations Officer]]s (SPOs).
- [[General Staff|General Staff Officer]]: The organized HQ staff aroun da senior commander, typically at the Division level or higher.
