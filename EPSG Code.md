Numeric identifiers for coordinate reference systems (CRS).

For instance, EPSG:4326 is the numeric identifier for the [[WGS84]] [[Coordinate Reference System|Coordinate Reference System]].

The EPSG Geodetic Parameter Dataset, also called the ==EPSG Registry==, is a public registry of geodetic datums, spatial reference systems, earth ellipsoids, coordinate transformations, and related units of measurements, originated from a member of the European Petroleum Survey Group in 1985.
- Each entity is assigned a EPSG code ==1024 and 32767== , along with a standard [[Well-Known Text|WKT]] representation.
- These days, the dataset is maintained by the IOGP Geomatics Committee (IOGP = International Association of Oil and Gas Producers)
