A company headquartered in Lynchburg, Virginia that supplies nuclear components and fuel internationally.

### Business Units

#### 1) BWXT mPower, Inc.
- In 2009, BWXT announced plans to design/develop/license/deploy a [[Small Modular Reactor|SMR]] called the BWXT mPower reactor, a passively safe 180MW reactor which was an integral [[Pressurized Water Reactor|PWR]] (iPWR) system in which nuclear core and steam generators were contained in a single vessel.
- Project terminated in 2017, it seems.

#### 2) BWXT Nuclear Energy, Inc.
- Manufactures nuclear components and provides engineering, design, construction, inspection, and repair services.
- The only North American company to continuously manufacture nuclear steam generators for the commercial nuclear power industry, since the 1950s.

#### 3) BWXT Nuclear Operations Group, Inc.
- Specializes in design and manufacture of large, heavy components for the US Government.
- ==Nuclear Fuel Services Inc (NFS)== is a subsidiary of BWXT NOG.
	- A major supplier of fuel for the US Navy's fleet of nuclear-powered vessels since the 1960s. Has reprocessed weapons-grade Uranium from the Cold War into nuclear reactor fuel, a process known as [[Downblending]].

#### 4) BWXT Technical Services Group, Inc.
- Manages complex, high-consequence nuclear and national security operations, including nuclear production facilities.
- ==Manages the nations Strategic Petroleum Reserve==, and provides a broad range of technical services to [[United States Department of Energy|DoE]] and the [[National Nuclear Security Administration]] (NNSA).
