Not a doctrinal unit in the military service, but it provides the logistical capabilities essential for troops on the front line.

Diverse team designed for a specific purpose and task organized to support the warfighter in their AO, e.g. supply, petroleum supply, ammunition, movement control, mortuary affairs, finance, postal.

In one example, it was organized under a [[Combat Sustainment Support Battalion]] (CSSB)
