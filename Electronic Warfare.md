The use of the [[Electromagnetic Spectrum]] (radio waves, microwaves, infrared, visible light, etc) and directed energy to control the spectrum, deny its use to adversaries, and ensure unimpeded access for friendly forces.
- The purpose is two-fold:
	- ==Deny the opponent the access to the EM Spectrum==
	- ==Ensure friendly, unimpeded access to the EM Spectrum==

Three Pillars of EW:
- ==Electronic Attack (EA)==: The offensive use of electromagnetic energy, directed energy, or anti-radiation weapons to degrade, neutralize, or destroy enemy combat capabilities
	- ==Jamming==: Broadcasting high-power signals to block or scramble enemy communications and radar.
	- ==Spoofing==: Transmitting fake signals to trick enemy navigation or drone systems into accepting incorrect coordinates.
- ==Electronic Protection (EP)==: Defense measures taken to ensure friendly forces can still use the EM spectrum, even while the enemy is attacking it.
	- ==Frequency Hopping==: Rapidly switching radio frequencies to avoid jamming.
	- ==Hardening== electronic equipment
- ==Electronic Support (ES)==: The intelligence-gathering phase. It involves intercepting, identifying, and locating electromagnetic emissions to provide commanders with immediate situational awareness regarding enemy threats and locations.
