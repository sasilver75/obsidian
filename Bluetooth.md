See also: [[Bluetooth Low Energy]] (BLE)

Shortrange wireless communication technology used to connect devices (headphones, keybords, mice, phones, watches, fitness trackers, medical devices, etc.).

Key points:
- Operates mainly in the 2.4 GHz band of the [[Radio Spectrum]].
- Designed for short-range, low-power communication.
- Supports device pairing and encrypted links.


- Compared with [[Bluetooth Low Energy]] (BLE)
	- BLE uses less power and is better for small bursts of data.
	- Bluetooth Classic is better for continuous high-bandwidth use like traditional audio streaming.
