"Aeronautical Reconnaissance Coverage Geographic Information System"

A commercial product from [[ESRI]], the dominant company is enterprise GIS.
More polished and powerful than [[QGIS]], and has deep integration with government and municipal workflows, costing thousands of dollars per license.

Most city GIS department (e.g. LA's) produce their data IN ArcGIS, which is why you'll frequently encounter `.shp` ([[Shapefile]]) formats - that's ESRI's legacy format.
