A commercial product from [[ESRI]], the dominant company is enterprise GIS.
More polished and powerful than [[QGIS]], and has deep integraiton with government and municipal workflows, costing thousands of dollars per license.

Most city GIS department (e.g. LA's) produce their data IN ArcGIS, whichi s why you'll frequently encounter `.shp` ([[Shapefile]]) formats - that's ESRI's legacy format.
