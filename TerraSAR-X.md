A [[Remote Sensing]] Earth observation satellite that's a public-private partnership between the German Aerospace Center (DLR) and EADS Astrium.

Launched in 2007 and operational since 2008.

With its twin satellite [[TanDEM-X]] (launched in 2010), it acquires the data basis for the [[WorldDEM]], the worldwide and homogenous [[Digital Elevation Model]]  available from 2014.

Uses a [[Synthetic Aperture Radar]] (SAR) antenna to provide radar images of the entire planet from an Earth polar orbit of 514km alitutde, selected so that the satellite follows a Sun-synch
