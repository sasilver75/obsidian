---
A military mission focused on reducing, disrupting, or temporarily neutralizing enemy air defense systems so that friendly aircraft, missiles, drones, or other forces can operate with less risk.

Can target or affect:
- Radar systems
- Surface to air missile systems
- Air-defense command posts
- Fire-control systems
- Communication links
- Anti-aircraft artillery
- Sensors and emitters

Doesn't always mean destroying the system, just become less effective for a period of time.
- For destruction, see [[Destruction of Enemy Air Defenses]] (DEAD).
