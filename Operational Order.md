A structured military order that tells a unit what mission to execute, why, when, where, and how.

Typically used to turn a commander's plan into clear instructions for subordinate users.

Typically has five main sections:
1. Situation: Enemy, friendly forces, terrain, weather, context.
2. Mission: Who, what, when, where, and why
3. Execution: Commander's intent, [[Concept of Operations]] (ConOp), tasks, and coordinating instructions.
4. Sustainment: Logistics, supplies, medical, transportation, maintenance.
5. Command and Signal: Leadership, communications, call signs, reporting, and control measures.

TLDR: The formal plan for carrying out an operation.
