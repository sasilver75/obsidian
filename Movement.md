The planned or active movement of people, equipment, or supplies from origin to destination.
"Movement Control" is the broader coordination process that schedules, routes, prioritizes, and regulates movements.

The planned or active act of moving a [[Logistics Package]] (LOGPAC) from one place to another.

Move specific than a [[Course of Action]]

Includes:
- Origin
- Destination
- Assigned convoy/prime movers
- Supply load
- Departure time
- ETA/required delivery time
- Selected route or route variant
- Status: proposed, approved, in progress, delayed, completed

Relationship:
- [[Course of Action]]
	- May include one or more Movements
- [[Movement]]
	- Uses one [[Logistics Package|LOGPAC]]/convoy
