aliases:
  - BOM
---
A structured list of parts, materials, components, [[Subassembly|Subassemblies]], or quantities needed to build/repair/support an item.

A BOM commonly inclucdes:
- Item name
- Part number or national stock number
- Quantity required
- Unit of issue, such as each/box/roll/gallon
- Description
- Manufacturer or approved source
- Subassembly relationship, meaning which larger component it belongs to
- Notes, such as substitutes, repair parts, or consumables
