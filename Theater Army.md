- USARPAC (Pacific)
- USARNORTH (North America)

- USAREUR-AF (Europe and Africa)
- USARCENT ("Central", meaning middle east and central/south Asia): Also known as the Third Army, the Army Service Component Command for [[US Central Command|USCENTCOM]].
