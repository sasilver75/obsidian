A [[Warfighting Function]]

The related tasks and systems that move and employ forces to achieve a position of relative advantage over the enemy, including tasks like moving, deploying, and maneuvering assigned forces across the operational area.

A [[Maneuver Unit]] is a military unit whose purpose is to move/fight/seize terrain, and close with the enemy. Typically, these are combat arms units like Infantry, Armor, Cavalry, Stryker units.
- Maneuver units typically work with support/enabling units, such as logistics, signal, medical, maintenance, intelligence, or administrative units.
