A modern [[Virtual Private Network]] (VPN) protocol.
- It creates an encrypted tunnel between two machines; think of it as a secure pipe: trafic goes in one end, gets encrypted, travels over the internet, and comes out hte other end decrypted.
- It's know for being extremely simple (~4000 LoC vs OpenVPN's ~100,000), fast, and built into the Linux kernel since 5.6.
