A geospatial intelligence company operating a constellation of small satellites to provide high-frequency [[Remote Sensing|Earth Observation]] imagery and analytics.
- They complete with [[Planet Labs|Planet]] and [[Maxar]] in the commercial EO space, but differentiate on high-revist tasking and integrated analytics, rather than raw archive size.

Characteristics
- Satellites: Operates a fleet of small (~55kg) low-Earth orbit optical imaging satellites
- Revisit Rate: Designed for frequent revisits to specific locations
- Analytics: Combines satellite imagery with AI/ML to deliver real-time monitoring and intelligence products.
- Customers: Primarily governments (defense, intelligence agency) and commercial clients needing persistent area monitoring.
- Platform: Their "Spectra" platform provides automated monitoring, change detection, and event alerting.
