A *distributed* sustainment area used by a [[Light Support Battalion]] (LSB) to support a [[Brigade Combat Team|Light Brigade Combat Team]] through multiple smaller [[Light Logistics Cluster]]s, rather than through a centralized [[Brigade Support Area]].

> A [[Light Support Battalion|LSB]] operates a [[Logistics Support Area|LSA]], which is made up of multiple [[Light Logistics Cluster|LLC]]s.

The idea is that in modern [[Large Scale Combat Operations]], a centralized [[Brigade Support Area|BSA]] (and the [[Brigade Support Battalion]] that staffs it) is too vulnerable to sensing/targeting from the enemy, so we'll instead create a dispersed set of [[Light Logistics Cluster|LLC]] nodes in an [[Logistics Support Area|LSA]], a distributed sustainment area operated by a [[Light Support Battalion]].
