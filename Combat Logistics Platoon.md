In the [[Light Support Battalion]] prototype concept, Combat Logistics Platoons are smaller, task-organized sustainment elements that replace some of what an old [[Forward Support Company]] did for a maneuver battalion.

A Combat Logistics Platoon is a forward distribution element from the Light Support Battalion that is task-organized to support a maneuver battalion or task force with supplies such as fuel, water, ammunition, food, and other [[Logistics Package|LOGPAC]]s. CLPs are the forward "push supplies to the fight" element.

See also: [[Combat Repair Team]] (CRT)


In the old [[Forward Support Company]] model:
- One FSC habitually supports a [[Battalion]] with distribution, maintenance, and feeding.

In the new prototype [[Light Support Battalion]] model:
- [[Combat Logistics Platoon]]s forward distribution
- [[Combat Repair Team]]s forward repair/maintenance
- The [[Light Support Battalion]] + [[Support Operations Officer]] centrally tasks and reallocates them by mission priority.
