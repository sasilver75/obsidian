An open-source organization that maintains a family of mapping libraries, created in 2021 when [[Mapbox]] changed the license on [[Mapbox GL JS]] v2 from open-source to proprietary. 
- The community forked it, forming hte MapLibre organization to maintain it independently.

[[MapLibre GL JS]] is the specific JS library
- GL means it uses [[WebGL]] to render the map on a GPU-accelerated [[Canvas]], which is why it's so smooth and fast.
