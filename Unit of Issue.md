In supply chain/logistics, U/I stands for Unit of Issue, and specifies the exact grouping or packaging in which a product is ordered and issued to a user.
- Each (EA)
- Box (BX)
- Case (CS)
- Yard (YD)
- Foot(FT)
- etc.
