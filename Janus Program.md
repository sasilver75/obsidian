Announced in November, 2025

Driven by EO 14288: `Deploying Advanced Nuclear Reactor Technologies for National Security`, the US Army's Janus program aims to deliver resilient, secure, and assured energy to support national defense installations and crucial missions.

Aims to use commercial [[Microreactor]]s in the <20MWe range and advanced fuels like [[Tri-Structural Isotropic Fuel|TRISO]] particles, which reduce probability and consequences of an uncontrolled release.
- The idea is to provide cost-effective, reliable energy with built-in safety features not available to current commercial reactors.
