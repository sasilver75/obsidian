The officer formally placed in command of a military unit, installation, ship, or organization.

Has legal and operational authority over the unit, including responsibility for:
- Mission accomplishment
- Training and readiness
- Discipline and good order
- Personnel welfare
- Equipment and property accountability
- Safety and standards
- Administrative and operational decisions

Examples:
- A [[Company]] commander is usually a captain
- A [[Battalion]] commander is typically a lieutenant colonel
- A [[Brigade]] commander is usually a colonel
- A [[Division]] commander is usually a major general
