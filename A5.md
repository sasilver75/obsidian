While [[H3]] was designed by Uber for ridesharing in cities, it's been taken and used in many contexts where it doesn't make sense.
- One particular weakness is that GLOBALLY, the variation in size is quite big: The variation between the largest and smallest cell is 2x!

Instead of projecting onto a cube ([[S2 Geometry|S2]]), icosahedron ([[H3]]), or octahedron (HTM), A5 uses a dodecahedron, the Platonic solid with the lowest vertex curvature, which minimizes distortion when mapping to a sphere.

Details:
- 31 resolution levels (entire world to <30mm^2)
- Cells encoded as 64-bit integers, like S2
- Truly equal-area at every resolution level, not just approximate
- Uses equilateral (but not regular) pentagons to tile the dodecahedron
