An open standards organization that designs the technical standards for how Internet systems communicate, such as:
- [[Transport Control Protocol]] (TCP)
- [[Internet Protocol]] (IP)
- [[Transport Layer Security]] (TLS)
- [[Domain Name Service]] (DNS)-related protocols
- [[Simple Mail Transfer Protocol]] (SMTP)
- Many others!

Its work is published mainly as RFCs, or "Requests for Comments." Some RFCs become official Internet standards.


# Comparison with [[Internet Assigned Numbers Authority]] (IANA)
- While IETF designs the technical rules for how internet systems communicate, IANA manages many of the numbers and registries those rules depend on.
